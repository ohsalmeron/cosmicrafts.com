## Token Distribution Strategy

Total Fixed Supply: **1,000,000,000 SPIRAL Tokens (1 Billion)**

To align incentives and protect early backers who supported our vision through the years, we have restructured the allocation to fully prioritize our seed investors while ensuring founders' tokens remain dedicated to scaling the MVP.

| Allocation Group | Percentage | Token Allocation | Purpose & Vesting Terms |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :--- |
| **Seed Investors** | **2.6%** | **26,000,000** | Fully preserved, non-diluted, and honored. |
| **Former Team** | **1.3%** | **13,000,000** | Capped at exactly half of the Seed Investors' share to protect investor significance. |
| **Founders & Treasury** | **96.1%** | **961,000,000** | Retained by founders and treasury, vested to guarantee server scaling, marketing, and market-making liquidity. |

## Why Ethereum (ERC-20)?

Deploying our token on Ethereum guarantees:
- **Maximum Liquidity**: Access to the largest decentralized exchanges (DEXs) like Uniswap.
- **Absolute Longevity**: Unlike sovereign subnet chains or cycle-reliant networks, Ethereum is a permanent utility ledger. Your tokens will exist as long as the Ethereum blockchain exists.
- **Simple Wallets**: Direct compatibility with standard mobile and browser wallets (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, etc.) for a friction-free claim and staking dashboard experience.
