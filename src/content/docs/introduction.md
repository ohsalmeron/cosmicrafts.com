## The Pivot

After years of pushing the boundaries of fully on-chain Web3 gaming ecosystems, we have reached a critical realization. Delivering a premium, zero-latency gaming experience alongside a stable, secure, and permanent token ledger requires extreme focus and architectural simplicity.

We are shifting our primary technical infrastructure:
- **Deprecating ICP canisters**: Managing complex, fully on-chain game state engines on Internet Computer canisters has proven to be high-maintenance, costly, and subject to severe cycle-depletion risks. 
- **Transitioning to High-Performance Cloud + Ethereum**: All matchmaking, player profiles, and multiplayer battle states are now being centralized on state-of-the-art hybrid cloud servers, eliminating in-game gas fees and latency. 
- **Standard ERC-20 Token**: The `SPIRAL` token will be launched on **Ethereum Mainnet**, guaranteeing the deepest liquidity, absolute developer transparency, and permanent ledger stability that will live forever without cycle management.

## The Mission

Our focus remains unchanged: delivering a fully functional, breathtaking space RTS game built for both competitive Web3 and mainstream players. By simplifying the stack, we are shrinking our operation to a highly efficient Minimum Viable Project (MVP) to successfully launch the token and reward our community and early backers.
